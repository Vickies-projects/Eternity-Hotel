Preparations:

    Here's our process for preparing our pizza:

  1. Ingredients Preparation:

    • Dough: You can use store-bought dough or make your own. If making it, mix flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil.
      Knead until smooth, let rise until doubled in size.
    • Sauce:Typically, tomato sauce mixed with herbs like oregano and basil. You can use store-bought or make your own.
    • Cheese:Mozzarella is commonly used, but you can add others like cheddar or parmesan.
    • Toppings:Prepare any vegetables, meats, or other toppings you like.

  2. Preheat Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 475-500°F (245-260°C) for a crispy crust.

  3. Shape the Dough:

    • On a floured surface, roll or stretch the dough into your desired shape and thickness.
      If you prefer a thicker crust, roll it out less thinly.

  4. Add sauce:

    • Spread a thin layer of sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.

  5. Add Cheese:

    • Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.

  6. Add Toppings:

    • Place your selected toppings evenly over the cheese.

  7. Bake:

    • Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
      Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
    .

  8. Cool and Serve:

    • Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing.
      This helps the cheese set and makes slicing easier.
